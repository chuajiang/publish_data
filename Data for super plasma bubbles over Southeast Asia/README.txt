# publish_data
Data for publish papers by Chunhua Jiang
Data and files in this folder are for publish of Observations of super plasma bubbles over South East Asia longitude sector. 
Puer.zip, Leshan.zip and Zhangye.zip files include ionograms recorded at Puer, Leshan and Zhangye Station, respectively. About how to read data files,  please see the document "AGU Supporting-Information.docx"
Please note that the time of ionograms is the Beijing Time (Local time=Beijing Time-1, Beijing Time = UT+8, Local time=UT+7).
